    Thought

    From an early age, I had a sense of service. I did small-to-large service activities. While I was in Rajkot and studying in S.Y. B.Sc. I used to join the activities of "Punit Seva Ashram". From then onwards, I had an idea that I would also do something like this and build an organization to provide such services.

    The "Man-Nirmal Foundation" was born to make this dream a reality. Service activities continued even without the organization. In Gondal, Giving green grass to cows, feeding the poor, feeding Biscuits, Ganthiya, Bundi in slums. All these activities I used to do with my Father. From then on, my mind turned to this, and the idea of establishing an organization came up.

    Objective

    The main objective of the organization is to eradicate the hunger of the poor by donating food. At the same time, to provide grass and fodder to the hungry animals, birds. And also to involve the people in the work of this social service.

    Supportance

    My family has already given their full support to the "Man-Nirmal Foundation". My mother Mrs. Manjulaben, and elder sister Mrs. Divyataben encouraged my thoughts and work. My wife Mrs. Lattaben, my son Mr. Jugesh and daughter-in-law Mrs. Hemakshi, have contributed physically and mentally.

    Besides, my friends Mr. Rajubhai Dharod, Mr. Subhashbhai Jain, Mr. Dipenbhai Dharod, and Mrs. Monikaben Dharod - all of them have fully supported me and the "Man-Nirmal Foundation" from the beginning. I am grateful to them.

    First Step

    The organization started in the year 2019 with "Chhas Vitran". In the hot summer of April-May, it started with the distribution of 20 liters of "Chhas(buttermilk)", and later it was distributed up to about 130 liters.

    Current Activities/Services

    • Giving bananas to monkeys every morning
    • Giving ganthiya to birds
    • Giving biscuits, milk and roti to dogs
    • Giving food to the poor who have no one, sitting on the sidewalk
    • Going to Nadiad Civil Hospital Feeding the hungry every Thursday

    Even during the lockdown's hard times due to the covid-19 epidemic, the Man-Nirmal Foundation continued to try to satisfy the hunger of the poor by donating food.

    Future Goals

    • In the future, To provide nutritious food to the hungry people in three government hospitals in Nadiad.
    • To provide food to the poor children in the government primary schools of Nadiad city.
      To cover the surrounding primary school children under the "Baalbhojan" project, if the government or a donor will provide further assistance and funding.
    • To provide educational assistance to poor children.
    • Trying to meet the small and big needs of the poor.
    • Increase service activities as future assistance is available.
