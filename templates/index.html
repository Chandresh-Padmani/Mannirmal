    The Man-Nirmal Foundation

    Man-Nirmal Foundation is a not-for-profit organization headquartered in Nadiad, Gujarat-India. Man-Nirmal strives to eliminate classroom hunger through mass food donation campaigning. It provides nutritious meals to volunteers, who then distribute them among the needy. Dr. Mukeshkumar Navnitrai Gandhi is the founder of the Man-Nirmal Foundation. He is a retired physics teacher from New English School, Nadiad. Dr. Gandhi and his team aim to eliminate hunger among kids.

    Our Vision

    No child shall be withheld from a good education because of hunger.

    Our Mission

    To feed 1 million needy people by 2030.

    Activities

    Find Out Our More Activities

    Serve Humans Food

    Volunteers from Man-Nirmal distribute meals to the needy. They provide meals ranging from lentil-rice to moong-dal polenta to neglected children and the elderly on the streets near them. Apart from gracing the needy with a full stomach, the activity also benefits the donor's physical, psychological, emotional, and spiritual well-being.
    Also, on the volunteer's birthday, the foundation pays for the food!

    Feed Animals

    "If having a soul means being able to feel love and loyalty and gratitude, then animals are better off than a lot of humans."
    Volunteers feed the starving strays that prowl the streets nearby. Dogs, cats, cows, monkeys, etcetera are fed appropriate foodstuffs like biscuits, fish, flatbread, bananas, and fruit.
    We aim to feed starving strays in our surroundings.

    Donate Blood

    Feel like you don’t have much to offer? You have the most precious resource of all: blood! Help share this invaluable commodity with someone in need. You need a big heart and a generous mind for blood donation. Give blood. Save a life.

      F.A.Q

      Frequently Asked Questions

      “Do some selfless service for people who are in need. Consider the whole picture, not just our little selves.”
      -Nina Hagen

      • Where does Man-Nirmal Foundation conduct its distribution activities?

        For now, the foundation only conducts distribution in Nadiad, Gujarat, India.

      • How old is the Man-Nirmal Foundation?

        The foundation was established in March 2019. Man-Nirmal is just 2 years old!

      • Who are the people behind Man-Nirmal Foundation?

        Mr. Mukeshbhai Gandhi, Mrs. Lattaben Gandhi, Mr. Jugesh Gandhi, Mrs. Hemaxi Gandhi, Mr. Rajesh Dharod, Mr. Subhashbhai Jain, Mr. Romil Patel, and Mr. Dipenbhai Dharod.

      • How do I become a part of Man-Nirmal?

        You can join us by becoming a volunteer. You can also donate to us, and we will then spread it through the world.

      • On average, how many people benefit through the actions of the Man-Nirmal Foundation?

        Man-Nirmal aids to 20-25 people daily. Additionally, we feed hungry animals and birds too.

      • What will Man-Nirmal do with my donation?

        Man-Nirmal uses 100% of your money to buy resources. These resources are segregated between volunteers for distribution among poor people and stray animals in the streets.
